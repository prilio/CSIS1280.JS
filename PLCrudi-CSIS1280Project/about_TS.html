welcome to our restaurant

A regular daily diet does contain enough protein and at Thunder Smoothies we want to meet your daily protein requirements. By getting your Thunder not only will you have a lot of energy, you will also be getting all the nutrients you need to get through your day. To serve healthy drinks free from preservatives and artificial sweeteners. We also offer an online ordering option so that people can order ahead of time and don't have to wait in line to get their orders as well as deliver through DoorDash and UberEats. Thunder Smoothies really values loyalty, therefore we offer our customers a loyalty program where after buying 4 smoothies they get the 5th one for free!



At Thunder Smoothies we really care about helping our less fortunate communities, therefore 1% of every sale will be donated to the local homeless shelter. This also encourages our customers to help their local communities when they buy smoothies from us.
